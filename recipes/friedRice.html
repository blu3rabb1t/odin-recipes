Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Description of yummy fried rice yayy :P

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Pepper

Steps

  • Cook rice
  • Scramble eggs
  • Chop carrots, peas, and green onions
  • Heat oil in a pan
  • Add garlic and vegetables
  • Add rice and eggs
  • Add soy sauce, sesame oil, and pepper
