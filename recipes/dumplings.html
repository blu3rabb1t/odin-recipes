Description

Description of yummy dumplings yayy :P

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Ground Pork
  • Green Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Pepper
  • Chili Oil
  • Wonton Wrappers

Steps

  • Mix flour and water to make dough
  • Roll out dough and cut into circles
  • Mix ground pork, green onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, pepper, and chili oil
  • Place filling in center of wrapper
  • Seal wrapper
  • Boil dumplings
  • Serve with soy sauce and chili oil
