Curry Recipe

Description

Description of yummy curry yayy :P

Ingredients

  • curry powder
  • coconut milk
  • chicken
  • potatoes
  • carrots
  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • pepper
  • rice
  • water
  • oil
  • salt
  • chicken

Steps

  • Heat oil in a pan
  • Add garlic, ginger, and onions
  • Add chicken and cook until brown
  • Add curry powder and cook for a few minutes
  • Add coconut milk and water
  • Add potatoes and carrots
  • Simmer until vegetables are cooked
  • Season with salt and pepper
  • Serve with rice
